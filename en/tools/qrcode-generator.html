Frequently Asked Questions

How to add a logo to a QR code?

Upload your logo image, and JieBang automatically embeds it at the center of your QR code with proper error correction (Level H) to maintain scannability.

Can I create a WiFi QR code?

Yes! Select the WiFi tab, enter your network name (SSID), password, and encryption type. Anyone scanning the QR code will auto-connect to your WiFi.

What's the difference between PNG and SVG QR codes?

PNG is a raster image best for digital use. SVG is a vector format that scales to any size without quality loss — ideal for printing on business cards, posters, or large displays.

Is this QR code generator free?

Yes, 100% free with no limits. Unlike many competitors that charge for logo embedding or high-resolution downloads, JieBang includes all features at no cost.

How to create a vCard QR code?

Select the vCard tab, fill in your contact details (name, phone, email, company), and a QR code is generated. Scanning it adds your contact directly to the phone's address book.
