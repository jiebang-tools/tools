Frequently Asked Questions

How to convert HEIC to JPG?

Upload your HEIC file (from iPhone), select JPG as output format, and click Convert. JieBang converts HEIC files entirely in your browser — no upload needed.

Can I convert multiple images at once?

Yes! Drag and drop or select multiple images at once. After conversion, download them individually or as a ZIP file.

Is my image data safe?

Absolutely. All processing happens locally in your browser using JavaScript. Your images are never uploaded to any server.

What's the difference between PNG and JPG?

PNG supports transparency and lossless compression, ideal for logos and screenshots. JPG uses lossy compression for smaller file sizes, best for photographs. WebP offers both — smaller than JPG with quality close to PNG.

Does this tool support HEIC files from iPhone?

Yes! JieBang is one of the few free tools that supports HEIC-to-JPG/PNG/WebP conversion. iPhone photos are saved as HEIC by default — convert them to share universally.
